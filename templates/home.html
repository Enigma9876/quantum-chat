Frequently Asked Questions

Insightful Encryptions is an interactive cryptographic learning platform where you can experiment with real cipher algorithms, send encrypted messages live across devices, and step through the full mathematical process of encryption and decryption at the character level.
No prior knowledge is required. Each cipher in the Lab comes with a built-in history guide, a live example preview, and a character-by-character breakdown table so you can understand exactly what is happening to your message at every step.
The platform supports Caesar shift, Hill matrix cipher, Vigenère keyword cipher, AES-256, and BB84 Quantum Key Distribution. Each one includes a live ciphertext preview, a Guide tab with history and mathematics, and an interactive Decrypt Tools tab with both an automated algorithm view and a manual walkthrough mode.
You host a room and share its code with anyone you want to communicate with. Each participant picks their own cipher and configures their key. Messages are encrypted locally on your device before they are ever transmitted. Anyone in the room can tap the inspect icon on any message to see the full encryption and decryption breakdown for that specific payload.
Yes. The host has a control panel that can force all participants to use a specific cipher, mute individual users or the entire room, kick participants, and rename users. Forced cipher restrictions lock the cipher selector for everyone except the host.
Messages exist only for the lifetime of the active room session in memory on the server. No message history or cryptographic keys are written to any permanent storage. When all users leave a room the session data is discarded.
The Algorithm view runs an animated step-by-step visualization of how a computer would break or reverse the cipher. The Manual view lets you work through each mathematical step yourself by entering answers and getting immediate feedback on whether your reasoning is correct.
